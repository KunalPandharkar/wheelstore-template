Frequently Asked Questions

Wheelstore is an online platform that helps you find nearby garages, service centers, showrooms, second-hand vehicle dealers, washing centers, workshops, and spare parts for your vehicle. We provide comprehensive information and ratings to help you choose the best services and products for your automobile needs.

Simply enter your location or city in location box, and Wheelstore will display a list of nearby service centers, garages, and other automotive services. You can filter the results based on ratings, and specific services offered.

Yes, you can find a variety of spare parts for different types of vehicles on Wheelstore. Just use our search feature to look for the specific parts you need, and you will see a list of shops and dealers that have them in stock.

We regularly update our database to ensure that the information provided is accurate and upto-date. Our team works diligently to add new shops and services and to remove those that are no longer in operation.

If you own an automotive shop or provide related services, you can add your business to Wheelstore by filling out the 'Add Your Shop' form on our website. Our team will review your submission and, once approved, your business will be listed on our platform.
